Basic information

This is the core identity of the campaign. Keep the title clear, the hook sharp, and the description vivid.

Examples: Epic, Political Intrigue, Dark Fantasy, Mystery, Beginner Friendly.

Image

Use a 16:9 banner. You can host it in your repo under /assets/campaigns/ and paste the path here.

Artwork reminder: only use art you have the rights to. If you commission work, add credit info below.

Search & social
